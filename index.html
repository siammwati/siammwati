Mercedes G-Wagon

The Mercedes-Benz G-Class, sometimes colloquially called the G-Wagen is a four-wheel drive automobile manufactured by Magna Steyr in Austria and sold by Mercedes-Benz

Originally developed as a military off-roader, later more luxurious models were added to the line.

Jeep Wrangler

The Jeep Wrangler is a series of compact and mid-size four-wheel drive off-road SUVs manufactured by Jeep since 1986, and currently in its fourth generation.

The most recent generation was unveiled in late-2017 and is produced at Jeep's Toledo Complex

Chevrolet Camaro

The Chevrolet Camaro is a legendary muscle car that debuted in 1966 as a rival to the Ford Mustang

It is expected to be discontinued after 2023, as GM shifts its focus to electric vehicles, making it a rare and coveted piece of automotive history
